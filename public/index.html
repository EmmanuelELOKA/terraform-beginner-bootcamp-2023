Finnish Pancakes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs.
  2. Add the milk and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift the flour and add the salt and sugar. Mix the dry ingredients.
  4. Gradually add the dry mixture to the egg and milk mixture, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  5. Finally, add the melted butter and mix until the batter is smooth.
  6. Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  7. Pour a ladleful of batter onto the hot skillet, spreading it out into a thin, even layer.
  8. Cook until the edges start to brown and the pancake can be flipped easily, then flip it over and cook the other side until golden brown.
  9. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter or oil to the skillet as needed.
  10. Serve the Finnish pancakes hot with your choice of toppings, such as jam, berries, or powdered sugar.

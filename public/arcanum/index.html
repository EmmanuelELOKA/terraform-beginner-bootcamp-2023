Getting Started

To begin your adventure in Arcanum, follow these steps:

  1. Install the game on your computer.
  2. Launch the game and create a new character.
  3. Explore the rich and immersive world of Arcanum, completing quests and interacting with its inhabitants.
  4. Have fun and enjoy your journey!
